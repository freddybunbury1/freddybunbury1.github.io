Freddy Bunbury

I study microbial interactions through measurable metabolic exchange, focusing on vitamin B12 as a model system to link genomic variation to community function.

Research

Interaction traits: vitamin B12 exchange
I quantify synthesis, release, and uptake traits across bacterial isolates to understand how metabolites are shared and how this shapes community structure.

Intrinsic traits: thermotolerance
I study how cyanobacteria adapt to temperature variation, linking genomic features to growth and environmental distributions.
